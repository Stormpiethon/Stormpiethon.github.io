Degree Objectives & Projects

Degree objectives are set for each degree program, outlining the knowledge, skills, and competencies that students are expected to acquire during their studies. These objectives serve as a roadmap for students, guiding their academic journey and ensuring they are well-prepared for their chosen field of study. The following projects demonstrate the application of these objectives in various domains.
