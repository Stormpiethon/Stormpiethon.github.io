Student Innovation Project (SIP)

My Student Innovation Project resulted in the creation of an application called "Argue Smarter." Its core concept is rooted in a psychological strategy often employed in marketing. This strategy focuses on eliciting an emotional response to enhance memory retention of information. "Argue Smarter" achieves this through an AI-powered feature. The AI crafts humorous insults, each containing three sophisticated vocabulary words. Subsequently, the app provides the definitions for these words. The intentional use of humor aims to forge a memorable learning experience, thereby improving the user's communication abilities.
